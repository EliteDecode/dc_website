Privacy Policy​

By becoming familiar with and following our policies, you'll help us maintain a safe environment for all, and be able to avoid interruptions that might come from inadvertent violations of our rules.

Some of our rules reflect local legal requirements, while others are based on our experience of how best to protect everyone using eBay services.

Delivery Connect (DC), respects your concerns about privacy. This Privacy Notice applies to the personal information we obtain through your DC account and its services and features (collectively, “Services”). This Privacy Notice provides information about how we may collect, use, and share your personal information in connection with our Services.

Information we collect

Your Delivery Connect account is your one-stop account to enjoy some of our services and features. When you first create a DC account, we may collect information such as your name, date of birth, phone number, password and email address. Once you set up your Delivery Connect account and log in, you can use the convenient features and we may collect and maintain information about you in various ways. The types of personal information we may obtain include the following:

Information You Provide Directly

You may have the option to save information to your Delivery Connect account, such as:

  • When you register your visa/bank card to your Delivery Connect account for easier payment options or when you save information such as your address for faster checkout when you make purchases. We encrypt sensitive information (like your visa/bank card details) for enhanced security; and

  • When you save your personal information (e.g., name, phone number, email, shipping address) to fill in and use for participating services. User IDs & passwords to log in are stored only on your device, and Delivery Connect does not collect or have access to this information.

Information We Collect While Using Specific Services

You also may provide us information when you contact customer service, your communications, including any information you provide regarding your identity and product or service you’re contacting us about, may be collected.
Package photos, description, address, phone number, and other delivery related information and instructions you provide are all used to fulfill services rendered. Delivery history is also saved for future delivery

  • Security: Your Delivery Connect- account uses GPS, we may collect information such as: your device’s physical location.

  • Account and service linking : Certain third party apps or services may allow you to link your account to that third party’s account for easy log in and to access enhanced features. Note that such third-party websites, apps, services, and features may operate independently from Delivery Connect and may have their own privacy notices or policies, which you should review to understand how they process your information.

  • Device and Service Information:
    i. Hardware information: such as model, device name, device configuration and settings, MCC (mobile country code); MNC (mobile network code)
    ii. Software information: such as version and configuration, and operating system version; and
    iii. Identifiers : such as IMEI, IP address, serial number, MAC address and other unique device or service identifiers (such as Android ID).

  • Log and Usage Information: Diagnostic, technical, error, and usage information such as time and duration of your use of the Services, traffic usage, and touch interaction.

  • Other information about your use of your Delivery Connect account, such as the features and services you use, purchase history, and how you interact with content offered through your Delivery Connect account.

How We Use the Information We Obtain

We may use the information we collect for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and the following:

  • To identify and authenticate you so you can use the Services;

  • To improve and customize your experience with the Services, such as delivering content and responses tailored to you and the way you interact with the Services;

  • To link your Delivery Connect account features or services;

  • To respond to your requests, inquiries, and instructions made through or about the Services;

  • For marketing and advertising purposes, such as to deliver advertising, including customized advertisements, sponsored content, and promotional communications;

  • With your separate consent, we may use your precise geolocation to notify you when you are near participating Delivery Connect or third-party stores offering benefits for other purposes explained at the time you provide your consent; and/or
    • We may use and combine the information we collect about you from our Services with data from other services or features you use, your devices, and other sources as well as information about your online activities on websites and connected devices over time and across third-party websites, devices, apps, and other online features and services.
